Short Bio

I am a first year Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at UC Berkeley, working with Prof. Miki Lustig. I graduated with honor from the department of Biomedical Engineering in Tsinghua University. From 2015-2018, I have been working on Magnetic Resonance Temperature Imaging, supervised by Prof. Kui Ying and working at The Advanced Theranostics and 3-D Imaging Laboratory (AT3D Lab), supervised by Prof. Hongen liao for my undergraduate thesis. My research interests lie in computational magnetic resonance imaging, signal processing, medical imaging and computer vision.
